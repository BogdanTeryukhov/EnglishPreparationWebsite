Travel and Transport

Brief Review

  • Catch up with

    • Reach the same point/level as

  • Check out

    • Leave the hotel; investigate

  • Drop off

    • Let someone get out of a vehicle; fall asleep

  • Get back

    • Return from a place

  • Go away

    • Go on holiday

  • Keep up with

    • Stay at the same point/level as

  • Make for

    • Go in the direction of

  • Pick up

    • Stop in a vehicle to give someone a lift

  • Pull in

    • Stop by the side of the road

  • Run over

    • Hit with a car

  • Set out/off

    • Start a journey

  • Take off

    • Leave the ground

  • Turn round

    • Go back in the opposite direction

  • Check in

    • Register at a hotel or an airport

  • See off

    • Go to a train station, etc, to see someone leave
